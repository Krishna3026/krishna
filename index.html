üîç INTRODUCTION TO CYBER CRIME

In today's digital age, where technology permeates every aspect of our lives, cyber crime has emerged as one of the most significant threats to individuals, businesses, and nations worldwide. This comprehensive website serves as an educational resource to understand the various facets of cyber crime, its impact on society, and the measures we can take to protect ourselves.

Cyber crime encompasses a wide range of illegal activities conducted through digital means, including hacking, identity theft, financial fraud, and cyber terrorism. As our dependence on technology grows, so does the sophistication and frequency of these digital threats.

Through this project, we aim to raise awareness about cyber security, educate users about common threats, and provide practical guidance on staying safe in the digital world. Knowledge is power, and understanding cyber crime is the first step toward protecting yourself and your digital assets.
