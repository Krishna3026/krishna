üìã REALISTIC CASE STUDIES

CASE STUDY 1: The CEO Email Scam

Scenario: A company's CFO receives an urgent email from the "CEO" requesting a wire transfer for a confidential acquisition.

Outcome: $250,000 transferred to fraudulent account

Lesson: Always verify unusual financial requests through multiple channels

CASE STUDY 2: The Tech Support Scam

Scenario: Elderly victim receives call from "Microsoft support" claiming their computer has viruses

Outcome: $5,000 paid for fake services, computer infected with malware

Lesson: Legitimate companies never call unsolicited about computer problems
