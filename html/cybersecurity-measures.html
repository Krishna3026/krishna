What is 2FA?

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security by requiring two different types of verification: something you know (password) and something you have (phone, token, or biometric).

Types of 2FA:

  • üì± SMS Codes: Text messages with verification codes
  • üîë Authenticator Apps: Google Authenticator, Authy
  • üîê Hardware Tokens: Physical security keys
  • üëÜ Biometrics: Fingerprint or facial recognition
