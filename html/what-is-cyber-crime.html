üìñ DEFINITION

Cyber crime refers to any criminal activity that involves a computer, networked device, or a network. It encompasses a wide range of illegal activities carried out using digital technology, including computers, smartphones, and the internet.

These crimes can target individuals, businesses, governments, or even critical infrastructure, causing significant financial, emotional, and social damage.
